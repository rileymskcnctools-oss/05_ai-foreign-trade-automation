### Straight Answers for Serious Buyers

**Q1: My market is price-sensitive. Can you reduce the cost by substituting the FSC handle?**
A: Absolutely. While FSC hardwood is a premium feature, we can offer standard ash or poplar handles which significantly lower the unit cost. We also have PP/TPR handle options if your audience prefers non-wood alternatives. Just let us know your target FOB price.

**Q2: Will the epoxy coating fade or scratch off during ocean freight?**
A: Not likely. After coating, the spade undergoes a baking process for hardness. We place protective foam sleeves between each blade in the master carton to prevent friction during transit. If transit damage occurs, it’s covered by our trade assurance.

**Q3: Can you mix two types of spades in one 1200 PCS order?**
A: Standard MOQ is 1200 PCS per single SKU (model/color). However, for long-term partners, we can split an order into 2 models (600 each) with a small 5% service surcharge. This helps you test different local market preferences easily.

**Q4: What is your actual factory delivery time after the sample is approved?**
A: For a first order of 1,200-3,000 pieces, 60 calendar days is standard. During peak season (October-March for spring stock), we recommend adding a 15-day buffer. We have never missed a peak-season shipment deadline for our contractual clients.

**Q5: Do you offer permanent rust prevention on the carbon steel?**
A: Carbon steel is high-tensile but requires maintenance. We apply rust-preventative oil under the epoxy coat. For full stainless steel, we recommend you look at our SS-series, but note that stainless spades sacrifice blade flexibility compared to this carbon steel model.
