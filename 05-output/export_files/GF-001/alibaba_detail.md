# FAQ

### Q1:
Can I order below the 500-piece MOQ for a trial order?

Answer:
Our standard MOQ is 500 pieces to maintain production efficiency and competitive pricing. However, we understand that first-time buyers may want to evaluate product quality before committing to a full order. Contact our sales team — we can often accommodate smaller trial quantities from existing inventory or combine your order with other customers' production runs.

### Q2:
Do you offer private labeling and custom color box design?

Answer:
Yes, we provide full OEM packaging services. Send us your artwork in AI or PDF format and our design team will prepare a print-ready layout within 48 hours. Custom color boxes add approximately 3-5 days to the standard 25-day lead time for plate making and color approval.

### Q3:
What is your quality control process?

Answer:
We operate a three-stage QC system: incoming material inspection (steel grade verification and ash wood moisture testing), in-process checks (hardness testing each batch to HRC 45-50 and lacquer adhesion tests), and final random sampling inspection before packing using AQL 2.5 standards. Detailed inspection reports are available for every shipment.

### Q4:
How do you ensure the wooden handles don't crack during transit?

Answer:
All ash wood handles are kiln-dried to 10-12% moisture content before assembly, preventing shrinkage and cracking. During packing, each color-boxed fork is positioned handle-up within the master carton, and we use corner-reinforced export cartons to protect against stacking pressure during ocean freight.

### Q5:
What payment terms do you accept?

Answer:
We accept T/T (30% deposit, 70% balance before shipment) for all orders. For repeat customers with established order history, we offer L/C at sight and, in some cases, OA 30 days after B/L date. We can discuss terms that align with your company's procurement policies.
