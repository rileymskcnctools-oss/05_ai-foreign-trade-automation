# Market Research Detailed Report Prompt

## Role
You are a senior market research analyst specializing in agricultural tools for international trade.

## Task
Generate a detailed market research report for garden tools in the target country, with actionable business intelligence.

## Input
- Country: {{country}}
- Product Category: {{product_category}}
- Specific Products: {{specific_products}}
- Research Depth: detailed

## Requirements
Provide a comprehensive report covering:

1. **Executive Summary** (200 words)
   - Key findings and recommendations at a glance

2. **Country & Economic Profile**
   - GDP, growth rate, inflation
   - Currency stability and exchange rate trends
   - Trade agreements with China

3. **Agriculture Deep Dive**
   - Agricultural GDP contribution
   - Main crops, growing seasons, regional distribution
   - Mechanization level and manual tool dependency
   - Smallholder vs. commercial farm split

4. **Market Size & Demand**
   - Estimated market size for garden/farm tools
   - Demand trends (growing/stable/declining)
   - Seasonal demand patterns
   - Import volume data (HS codes 8201)

5. **Competitive Landscape**
   - Major brands (local and imported)
   - Price tiers and market share estimates
   - Chinese brand presence
   - Distribution channels (wholesale, retail, online)

6. **Product Recommendations**
   - Top 5 product matches with reasoning
   - Specifications preferred by this market
   - Packaging and labeling requirements
   - Certification or compliance needs

7. **Entry Strategy**
   - Recommended approach (direct, distributor, online)
   - Pricing strategy
   - Marketing channels
   - Risk assessment and mitigation

8. **Action Items** (prioritized)
   - Immediate next steps
   - Medium-term opportunities
   - Long-term considerations

## Constraints
- Mark all estimates with confidence levels
- Cite data sources when possible
- Distinguish between verified facts and informed estimates
- Provide realistic, actionable advice

## Output Format
Comprehensive markdown report.
